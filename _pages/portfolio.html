A collection of data analysis projects demonstrating end-to-end analytical workflows — from data cleaning and exploratory analysis to model fitting and interpretation. Methods span linear and logistic regression, classification, model selection, ensemble learning, SVM, PCA, clustering, survival analysis, mixed-effects models, GEE, causal inference, categorical modeling, Poisson regression, nonparametric density and curve estimation, Monte Carlo simulation, MCMC, EM algorithm, numerical optimization, Gaussian processes, and spline regression.

Baseball Analytics: Umpire Strike Zone Bias in WBC 2026 Taiwan vs. Australia

Kernel density estimation of the home plate umpire's practical strike zone reveals a striking asymmetry between teams — with Taiwan batters facing a significantly expanded zone.

Baseball Analytics: Dissecting the 2nd Inning Meltdown — Taiwan vs. Japan, WBC 2026

Was Cheng really to blame for the 10-run disaster? Using pitch-level data, Random Forest, and GBM models to decompose the damage and propose an optimal pitching strategy.

Nonparametric Curve Estimation: Density Estimation, Kernel Regression, Splines, and Local Methods

Histogram and kernel density estimation, bandwidth selection, multivariate KDE, local polynomial regression, smoothing splines, penalized regression, cross-validation, and GAMs.

Statistical Computing: Simulation, Monte Carlo, MCMC, Optimization, EM Algorithm, and Gaussian Processes

Random variate generation, Monte Carlo integration, MCMC sampling, bootstrap and permutation tests, Newton-Raphson optimization, EM algorithm, GP regression, and Bayesian optimization.

Linear Models: Regression Theory, Diagnostics, Weighted Least Squares, and Time Series Modeling

EDA, model fitting and selection, hypothesis testing, diagnostics, confidence regions, WLS, GLS with AR(1), polynomial regression, splines, and changepoint models.

Categorical Data Analysis: GLMs, Logistic Regression, Poisson Models, and Correspondence Analysis

Linear regression diagnostics, logistic regression for disease risk, Poisson regression with overdispersion, log-linear models, correspondence analysis, and symmetry testing.

Causal Inference and Biological Data Analysis: DAGs, GLMs, Mixed-Effects Models, and Treatment Effect Estimation

DAG-based causal reasoning, Poisson GLM with offset, logistic regression with ROC/calibration, mixed-effects models, GEE, meta-analysis, IPTW, and doubly robust estimation.

Survival Analysis: Nonparametric Estimation and Censored Data Modeling

Kaplan-Meier estimation, hazard functions, log-rank tests, and Cox proportional hazards models applied to clinical and reliability data.

Cost of Living Analysis: Global City Comparison via Multivariate Methods

PCA, factor analysis, and clustering applied to global cost-of-living data across 55 expenditure categories to identify city groupings and spending patterns.

SVM & Unsupervised Learning: High-Dimensional Image Classification

Support vector machines with PCA-based dimensionality reduction, t-SNE visualization, and clustering for casting defect detection.

Tree-Based & Ensemble Methods: Bike Sharing and Airline Satisfaction

Decision trees, random forests, gradient boosting, and nonlinear regression for demand prediction and customer satisfaction classification.

Model Selection & Regularization: Chemical Manufacturing Yield Prediction

Systematic comparison of subset selection, PCR, Lasso, and Ridge regression on high-dimensional manufacturing data.

Classification Methods: Breast Cancer Diagnosis

Comprehensive comparison of logistic regression, discriminant analysis, and KNN for tumor diagnosis.

Regression Modeling: Ozone Prediction & Prostate Cancer Analysis

Multiple regression analysis with model diagnostics, variable selection, and scientific interpretation on environmental and biomedical data.
