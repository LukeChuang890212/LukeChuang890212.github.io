## Graduate Research

<p style="color: #888; font-size: 0.9em; margin-bottom: 15px;">Institute of Statistics and Data Science, National Tsing Hua University</p>

<div style="background: #f8fafc; border-left: 5px solid #2563eb; padding: 20px; border-radius: 8px; margin: 20px 0;">
  <h3 style="color: #1e3a5f; margin-top: 0;">Ensemble-Based Multiply Robust Methods for Missing Not at Random Data with Model Misspecification</h3>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 8px; color: #888; font-size: 0.9em;">Spring 2023 – Present · Advisor: Prof. Yu-Jen Cheng</p>
  <p style="color: #475569; line-height: 1.7; margin-bottom: 10px;">
    Proposed a novel ensemble method for missing not at random data, combining multiple propensity score models with generalized methods of moments to create multiply robust inverse probability weighting estimators. Demonstrated its effectiveness under mild misspecification scenarios through simulation studies and real-world application to mental health data.
  </p>
  <span style="display: inline-block; background: #fef3c7; color: #92400e; padding: 4px 12px; border-radius: 20px; font-size: 0.85em; font-weight: bold;">Under review at Statistics in Medicine</span>
</div>

## Undergraduate Research

<p style="color: #888; font-size: 0.9em; margin-bottom: 15px;">Department of Psychology, National Cheng Kung University</p>

<div style="background: #ffffff; border: 2px solid #8b5cf6; border-left: 6px solid #8b5cf6; padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; margin-bottom: 18px;">
  <h3 style="color: #4c1d95; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 6px;">VR Training and Baseball Pitching Performance under Pressure</h3>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 8px; color: #888; font-size: 0.9em;">Spring 2021 – Spring 2022 · Advisor: Prof. Kwang-Yu Cheng (Institute of Physical Education, Health & Leisure Studies, NCKU)</p>
  <p style="margin: 0; color: #666; font-size: 0.95em; line-height: 1.7;">Investigated the effect of varying situational pressure levels on the pitching performance of college baseball players using a self-designed virtual reality training system, and explored the potential advantages of VR technology in enhancing players' resilience to pressure-filled situations.</p>
</div>

<div style="background: #ffffff; border: 2px solid #f59e0b; border-left: 6px solid #f59e0b; padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; margin-bottom: 18px;">
  <h3 style="color: #92400e; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 6px;">Factors Affecting CPBL Audience Attendance</h3>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 8px; color: #888; font-size: 0.9em;">2020 · Advisor: Prof. Po-Hsien Huang</p>
  <p style="margin: 0; color: #666; font-size: 0.95em; line-height: 1.7;">Investigated the impact of team matchups and weather conditions on audience willingness to attend Chinese Professional Baseball League games, using box office performance as a proxy, with data sourced from the official CPBL website and the Central Weather Bureau in Taiwan.</p>
</div>

<div style="background: #ffffff; border: 2px solid #0d9488; border-left: 6px solid #0d9488; padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; margin-bottom: 18px;">
  <h3 style="color: #134e4a; margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 6px;">Reward and Attentional Focus via Systems Factorial Technology</h3>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 8px; color: #888; font-size: 0.9em;">2019 · Advisor: Prof. Cheng-Ta Yang</p>
  <p style="margin: 0; color: #666; font-size: 0.95em; line-height: 1.7;">Collected data from an experimental psychology paradigm with a multi-factor design to investigate how rewards influence attentional focus by modeling the underlying information processing mechanisms through SFT.</p>
</div>
